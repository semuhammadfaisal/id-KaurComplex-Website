About Us

Welcome to Kaur Complex – A Vision for Modern Family Living in Peshawar

Kaur Complex is a thoughtfully planned residential project dedicated to providing a safe, comfortable, and modern lifestyle for families in Peshawar. Strategically located, the complex offers a secure gated community environment that reflects international living standards with a focus on family values and long-term investment potential.

We are proud to introduce a state-of-the-art residential and commercial development featuring:

  • 600+ International Standard Apartments & Condominiums
  • Luxury Penthouses with Scenic Views
  • High-End Commercial Shops for Modern Retail Needs

Each unit is designed with attention to space, aesthetics, and functionality—ensuring an ideal living experience for residents. Our goal is to create more than just housing—we are building a lifestyle, a community, and a future.

At Kaur Complex, quality construction meets elegant design, backed by a team committed to excellence in real estate development. Whether you're looking for a home to raise your family or a valuable investment opportunity, Kaur Complex is your trusted destination in Peshawar.
