Project Overview

Kaur Complex represents the pinnacle of luxury living in Peshawar, offering an unprecedented blend of modern architecture, premium amenities, and strategic location. Our development spans over 15 acres of prime real estate, featuring more than 600 meticulously designed residential units.

Built to international standards, Kaur Complex incorporates cutting-edge construction techniques, sustainable design principles, and world-class finishes. Each apartment is crafted with attention to detail, ensuring maximum comfort, functionality, and aesthetic appeal.
