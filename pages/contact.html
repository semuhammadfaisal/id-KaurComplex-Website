Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Kaur Complex

What are the payment plans available?

We offer flexible payment plans including cash payment (with 5% discount), installment plans (20% down payment, 24 monthly installments), and bank financing options.

When will the project be completed?

The project is scheduled for completion in December 2024. Currently, we are 80% complete and on track for timely delivery.

Can I schedule a site visit?

Yes, you can schedule a site visit by calling us or filling out the contact form. We offer guided tours Monday through Saturday.

What amenities are included?

Kaur Complex includes 24/7 security, swimming pools, fitness center, kids play area, mosque, grocery mart, parking, and many more premium amenities.

Is financing available?

Yes, we have partnerships with major banks offering competitive financing options with as low as 15% down payment and up to 10 years financing.

What is the maintenance fee?

Maintenance fees vary by apartment size and range from PKR 5,000 to PKR 15,000 per month, covering all amenities and services.
